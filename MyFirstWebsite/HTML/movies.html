Favorite Movies

I don't watch movies often, because I have a short attention span and usually miss important moments in the
movie because I'm distracted. However, I do enjoy watching movies from time to time! Below are a few of my
favorites.

The first is Lupin III: The Castle of Cagliostro. It was made in 1979, and has incredible animation, pretty
backgrounds, wonderful characters and a fun story. I highly recommend it, especially to those who like
Disney or Studio Ghibli movies!!



The second movie I really enjoy is Fantastic Mr. Fox! The story is hilarious, the stop-motion is a joy to
watch, and the characters are genuienly enjoyable. It's definteley more of a mellow movie, but I love it
all the same.



The last movie I really like, and probably my favorite, is The Shape of Water. It's a multi-award
winning movie, winning for best picture, best director, best original music score, and best production
design. I love this movie to bits, and could watch it anytime. It's directed by Guillermo del Toro, the
same man behind Hell Boy, Pan's Labrynth, and more.
