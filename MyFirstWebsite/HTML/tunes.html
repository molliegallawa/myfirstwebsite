Favorite Tunes

I listen to music a lot. Music has become a part of my daily life by now. I listen to it while working, playing
games, drawing, sleeping, etc! I don't think I could live without music.

The first band I love is The Gorillaz. I grew up with them, my dad often playing their music in the car. My
favorite songs of theirs are Humility, Feel Good Inc., Clint Eastwood, and Andromeda.

Below is one of their newest songs, Fire Flies, which is a new favorite for me.



The second band I adore is Passion Pit! I first found out about them when I played the game Little Big
Planet. Passion Pit's song, Sleepyhead, was one of the songs in the game's soundtrack, and ever since
I heard it I fell in love.


Below is the song that made me fall in love with this group, Sleepyhead.




The last band I adore is Glass Animals. I just recently found them about a year or two ago. They're an
indie band that has a really really cool sound, they use lots of drums and bass. They're probably my #1
favorite band!

And here's my favorite song of theirs. It's called Life Itself.
