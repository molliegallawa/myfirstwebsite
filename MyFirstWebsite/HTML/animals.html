Favorite Animals

I love animals so so much! I couldn't pick just 3 favorites, so here's 4 of my absolute favorite animals.

The first is a pomeranian, the cutest dog breed in the world, I swear. They're totally not my favorite because I have
one! Below are some pictures. The first picture is of my dog, Caesar!




Another animal I really like are axolots!! They're cute little salamanders with fluffy little antenna on either sides of
their heads. They come in pink, black, gold, and white!



Yet another animal I really love are fennec foxes. They are tiny, but have giant, almost disproportionate ears,
which makes them all the more adorable.



My last favorite animal is the Rosy Maple Moth. They are pink, yellow, and fluffy, and they're too cute.
