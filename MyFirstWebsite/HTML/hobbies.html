My Hobbies

I have a few hobbies, including drawing (both digitally and traditionally,) playing video games, and listening to music! I draw about
70% of the time, and the other 30% is playing video games, all while listening to my favorite tunes! I also loooove reading comics,
my favorite being the Transformers: More Than Meets The Eye series, as well as its currently-ongoing sequel, Lost Light.

Below are some of my digital drawings. I'm very proud of these! Each took an hour or more to finish, the fastest one being the first, as it's just
a sketch. A fully colored, shaded, and lined piece like the one on the right usually takes me 3-5 hours. I draw on a Huion610 Pro tablet, and
use FireAlpaca as a drawing program.



My favorite games to play are usually Nintendo games, as they're my favorite gaming company. I play on both my Switch and 3DS a lot, but I
also play on my PS4 if I feel like playing Overwatch. My favorite games are any Mega Man game, pretty much any Pokemon game (however
Ultra Moon and Ruby happen to be my favorite,) Sonic Mania and Sonic Adventure 2, Splatoon 2, and Animal Crossing: New Leaf, to name
a few.



Lastly, I love to read comics. Generally I hate reading, but having incredible art to look at as you read is a real
treat. I, in particular, love the Transformers: More Than Meets The Eye and Lost Light series. I've reread it multiple
times now, and issues are still coming out!!
